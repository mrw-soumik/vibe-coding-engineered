# MVP-002: Implement Human-in-the-Loop Review and Approval for Customer Communications

**Priority:** High  |  **Story points:** 5

**Suggested branch:** `feature/mvp-002-review-approval`

## User story
As a small ecommerce store owner, I want to have human-in-the-loop review and approval for all customer communications so that I can ensure personalized and accurate messages.

## Description
Implement a review and approval workflow for customer communications, including email templates and follow-up reminders.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Review and approval workflow is implemented for customer communications
- [ ] Email templates are customizable and approved by store owners
- [ ] Follow-up reminders are sent automatically after review and approval

## Definition of done
- [ ] Code is reviewed and approved by a senior developer
- [ ] UI/UX is optimized for review and approval workflow
- [ ] Integration with email list management is complete
